About Wealthyvision and how the name came about

wealthyvision is a name that has alot of meaning to it. It means the vision of wealth that he sees in the near future. It represents what he wants out of life and how he aspires to live generally. "Wealthyvision can mean alot of things" says the man himself. it can be music, sports, video, gaming, water bottles, dog food. its not limited to just one possibility.

wealthyvision has started out making beats from an ipad using an app called the iMPC. He has a huge interest in sampling and has made quite compelling beats using this method. his recent EP 'Hard Work Pays Off' is a reflection of the hard work that he has put in for a short period of time on an amazing body of work. Wealthyvision isn't seeing the amount of love due to lack of publicity but taking a look on his Soundcloud would make all of the difference. He has a team of producers by his side to help aid him in where he wants to take his musical imagination.
